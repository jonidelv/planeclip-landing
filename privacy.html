Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Plain-Language Summary

Planeclip is a flight-tracking app. We need some information to show you real-time flight data, but we keep data collection to a minimum. We do not sell your personal information. We do not show you ads. Your flight details and history are private to you.

What We Collect

When you use Planeclip, we may collect the following:

  • Flight data you enter — flight numbers, dates, booking references, and seat information you add to the app.
  • Email-imported flights — if you forward booking confirmation emails, we extract flight details and discard the rest of the email.
  • Account information — if you create an account, your email address and display name.
  • Device information — device model, OS version, and app version for debugging and compatibility.
  • Usage analytics — anonymous, aggregated data about how features are used (see Analytics below).

Analytics

We use privacy-friendly analytics to understand which features are popular and to fix bugs. Analytics data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you personally. We do not use third-party analytics services that track you across apps or websites.

Advertising

Planeclip does not display advertisements. We do not share your data with ad networks or data brokers.

How Your Information Is Used

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide real-time flight tracking, notifications, and alerts.
  • Display your flight history and statistics.
  • Enable live flight sharing when you choose to share.
  • Improve the app and fix bugs.
  • Respond to support requests.

How We Protect Your Information

Your data is transmitted over encrypted connections (TLS). Data stored on our servers is encrypted at rest. We follow industry-standard security practices and regularly review our infrastructure.

Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at planeclip@gmail.com. You may also delete your account and all associated data directly within the app.

California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, request its deletion, and opt out of its sale. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your rights, contact us at planeclip@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Planeclip is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

European Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under the GDPR, including the right to access, rectify, port, and erase your data, as well as the right to restrict or object to processing. Our legal basis for processing is your consent and our legitimate interest in providing and improving the service. To exercise your rights, contact planeclip@gmail.com.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including standard contractual clauses where required.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email. Continued use of Planeclip after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at planeclip@gmail.com.
